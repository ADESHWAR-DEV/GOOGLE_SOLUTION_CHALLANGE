# SportShield Investor & Judge Pitch

## Executive Summary

**SportShield** is the first comprehensive digital sports media protection platform, combining AI-powered detection, blockchain ownership, and C2PA content provenance to protect athletes, creators, and sports organizations from unauthorized use of their content.

### B. Key Metrics Definitions

- **GMV**: Gross Merchandise Value - total transaction volume
- **ARR**: Annual Recurring Revenue - subscription revenue annualized
- **LTV**: Lifetime Value - total expected revenue per customer
- **CAC**: Customer Acquisition Cost - total sales/marketing spend per customer
